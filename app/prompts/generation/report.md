# Prompt: Report Generation

## Objective

Generate a comprehensive candidacy analysis report from the analysis results.

## Report Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
- Candidate name and current title
- Job title and company
- Overall recommendation with score
- Top 3 strengths and top 3 concerns

### 2. Skills Alignment Matrix
- Table comparing required vs. demonstrated skills
- Visual indicators: aligned / partial / gap / exceeded

### 3. Experience Assessment
- Relevance of past roles to the position
- Progression trajectory
- Industry alignment

### 4. Gap Analysis
- Critical gaps that may block candidacy
- Significant gaps requiring discussion
- Minor gaps that can be trained

### 5. Signals & Opportunities
- Strengths to highlight in interview
- Risks to investigate further
- Opportunities for the candidate

### 6. Uncertainties
- Items requiring verification
- Suggested interview questions

### 7. Recommended Next Steps
- Concrete actions based on the analysis
